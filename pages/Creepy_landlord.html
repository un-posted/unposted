Creepy Landlord

By Klm | August 14, 2025

(So the names in this story are not used for privacy reasons)

Ever since I was little my family was always poor we lived in a small house and 2 years after living there we had to move my mom found a small 2 bedroom duplex right down the street I've never lived in a duplex so I didn't know how to feel but we kinda didn't have a choice we needed a place to stay so we moved in not to long after.

I have a twin sister so me and her had to share a room and my 2 brothers had to share one while my mom had to sleep on the couch at first the landlord seemed nice and like your average old sweet guy until one day I come home from school with my sister and my mom is talking to the landlord (the school was right down the block so I could walk home from there with sister alone)

The landlord looked at me and said:

"Your girls’ room is dirty, you need to clean it."
At first I thought my mom told him he could go in there since he needed a extension cord but the look on my mom's face said differently I let it go me being a 12 year old I went inside a week or so later I come home to my mom she is angry come to find out that my mom had showerd and since she was home alone she just walked around our duplex in a towel

She told me that she went to my room so she could take a nap with just her towel on since she didn't have a bedroom and she woke up to a package on the dining room table she thought my older brother had brought it in but she asked him and it was the landlord so he basically used his key to open the door and leave a package on the kitchen table

What happened a few weeks later shattered my view on men…

One of the other tenants were leaving the duplex and to be nice me and my mom were cleaning out the old apartment which was right above ours (you had to go outside to access the 2nd apartment) my mom's back was turned and she was cleaning some floor bords and I was looking out of the window down into the street when I felt a tap on my rear end I turn around and he has a curtain rod so he tapped my but with a curtain rod wen I looked at him he whisperd

"I bet an older guy has never touched you with a 10 foot pole like that before."

Creepy landlord, let’s not meet again.
